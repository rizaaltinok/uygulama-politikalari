Privacy Policy

Cep Link · Last updated: February 2025

This privacy policy describes how your personal data is collected, used, and protected when you use the Cep Link mobile application (“App”). By using the App, you accept this policy.

1. Data Controller

The data controller for the Cep Link app is the individual or entity that publishes and operates the App. Contact details may be provided on the app store listing or within the App.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account and identity information

  • Email address — For registration and sign-in.
  • Password — Stored in hashed form; we do not store plain-text passwords.
  • First name, last name, display name — Optional; for profile and personalisation.
  • Profile photo (avatar) URL — Optional; for visual identification linked to your account.

2.2 App content

  • Saved links — URL, title, description, category, notes, tags, favourite status, and preview image URL if available.
  • Categories — Category names and order you create.
  • Price tracking data — Price information for products you track and, if applicable, notification preferences.

2.3 Subscription and payment

  • Premium subscription status — Plan type (monthly/yearly), expiry date.
  • Purchase records — Product ID, transaction ID, platform, purchase and expiry dates. We do not collect payment card or bank details; payments are processed by Google Play.

2.4 Technical and usage data

  • Device and app information — Operating system, app version; for troubleshooting and compatibility.
  • Advertising identifiers — For free users, ad-related data such as advertising IDs may be processed by Google AdMob in accordance with AdMob policies.
We do not collect location data. The App does not request location (GPS) permission and does not collect your location. Time zone information is used only for scheduling notifications and is not linked to your device’s physical location.

3. Where Data Is Stored and Processed

  • On your device (local) — Links, categories, and preferences are stored in a local SQLite database for offline use.
  • In the cloud (Supabase) — When you sign in, account data, profile, links, and categories are processed on Supabase infrastructure for sync. Data may be held in EU or other compliant regions depending on Supabase configuration.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account and keep it secure.
  • To sync links and categories, back up data, and support use across devices.
  • To provide price tracking and deliver notifications on time.
  • To manage Premium subscriptions and maintain purchase history.
  • To check app version and provide update information.
  • To show ads to free users (Google AdMob).
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect legitimate interests.

5. Sharing With Third Parties

  • Supabase — Authentication, database, and cloud storage; they act as a data processor under appropriate agreements.
  • Google Play — App distribution and in-app subscriptions or one-time purchases; payment data stays with Google; we do not receive payment card details.
  • Google AdMob — Ad serving for free users; ad data is subject to Google’s privacy policy.

We do not sell your data to third parties, except where required by law or court order.

6. Data Retention

  • Account and synced data are kept while your account is active.
  • When you request account deletion, your personal data and account-linked content are deleted or anonymised, subject to any legal retention requirements.
  • Subscription and purchase records may be retained for a period required by tax or consumer law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access — Know what personal data we process about you.
  • Rectification — Have inaccurate or incomplete data corrected (you can also update profile and in-app settings).
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your data by deleting your account.
  • Data portability — Receive your data, including via in-app export.
  • Object and restrict — Object to or request restriction of certain processing where the law provides.

You can use in-app options such as “Delete account” or “Sign out”, or contact the data controller to exercise these rights.

8. Security

We protect your data with technical and organisational measures: passwords are stored using secure hashing, and communication is encrypted (TLS/HTTPS). Supabase and Google apply industry-standard security measures.

9. Children

Cep Link is not directed at individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes may be communicated via the app store or in-app notice. The current text will always be available at this page (or at the URL indicated in the app store).

11. Contact

For questions or requests about privacy and your personal data:

  • Use the developer contact information on the app store listing, or
  • Use the contact details provided in the App under “Profile” or “Help”.
